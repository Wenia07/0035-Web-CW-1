History

Back in 1904, the founders harnessed vacuum insulation technology to create the very first Thermos® product—a product that soon became essential for adventurers, explorers, and everyday people alike. Over a century later, the Genuine Thermos® Brand continues its dedication to delivering dependable products, remaining committed to helping you Protect What You Love™.
